How can we help?

Everything you need to know about preparing with AI Prep.

What is AI Prep?

AI Prep is an advanced interview preparation platform that uses AI to conduct realistic mock interviews across hundreds of tech roles. We provide instant feedback, scoring, and actionable insights to help you improve.

Is my data secure?

Yes, security is our top priority. All interview transcripts and audio recordings (if used) are encrypted. We never share your personal data or interview performance with employers without your explicit consent.

How does the AI provide feedback?

Our AI analyzes your answers for technical accuracy, communication clarity, confidence, and keyword usage. It then compares your responses against industry standards and provides specific suggestions for improvement.

Do you have a free tier?

Yes! You can start practicing for free. We offer a selection of basic mock interviews and feedback summaries to help everyone get started on their career journey.

Our support team is always ready to assist you with any inquiries or technical issues.
