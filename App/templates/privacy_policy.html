Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our top priority at AI Prep.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to our users. This includes:

  • Personal information (name, email) provided during signup.
  • Interview data, including audio recordings (if used) and text transcripts.
  • Usage data and performance metrics to improve our AI models.
  • Technical data like IP addresses and browser information.

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To provide personalized interview feedback and scoring.
  • To improve our AI algorithms and question sets.
  • To communicate with you about your account and updates.
  • To ensure the security and integrity of our platform.

3. Data Protection

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information:

  • End-to-end encryption for all sensitive data transmissions.
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments.
  • Strict access controls for our internal data processing systems.
  • Anonymization of data used for training AI models.

4. Your Rights

You have full control over your data on AI Prep:

  • The right to access and download your interview history.
  • The right to correct or delete your personal information.
  • The right to opt-out of data usage for AI training purposes.
  • The right to terminate your account at any time.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact our data protection team via our Contact Page.
